Corn Suitability Analysis

A GIS-based site suitability project to identify optimal areas for cultivating corn in Missouri, based on four key factors:

  • Land Use – appropriate for agriculture
  • Soil Drainage – well-drained soils
  • Slope – flat terrain is preferred
  • Distance to Roads – not too close, not too far

Tools Used

ArcGIS Spatial Analyst Raster Calculator Euclidean Distance

Suitability Equation

(LU_RECLASS * 2 + SOIL_RECLASS * 3 + SLOPE_RECLASS * 1 + ROAD_RECLASS * 2) / 8

This weighted raster overlay assigns scores from 1 (unsuitable) to 5 (excellent).
